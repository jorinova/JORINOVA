🔐 Voice Biometric Enrollment

Your unique voice will be used to verify your identity before executing voice commands. No raw audio is stored — only a mathematical voice fingerprint (embedding vector).

✅ Who can enroll:
  • Lab managers & pathologists
  • Laboratory technicians
  • Doctors & nurses
  • Receptionists & phlebotomists
  • All permanent staff
🚫 Who CANNOT enroll:
  • Interns & students
  • Visitors & observers
  • Temporary / guest accounts
  • Anyone using a shared login
⚠️ Important: Record in your normal working environment. Do NOT speak louder or softer than usual — the system learns your natural voice. Find a quiet space with no background music.
Privacy notice: Your voice recordings are processed locally. Only a mathematical embedding (256 numbers) is stored — never the raw audio. Your voiceprint cannot be reverse-engineered to recover your voice. Enrollment requires lab manager approval.
